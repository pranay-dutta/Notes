 What is **binary exponentiation**
 
---
**Positive power**

● **when power is even** $x^8$  = $(x*x)^8/2$
● **when power is odd**  $x^9$  = $x * ((x*x)^8/2$ )

Generalized
$pow(x, n)$ = $pow(x*x, n/2)$

---
**Negative power**

$x^-8$ = $1/x^8$ = $(1/x)^8$
